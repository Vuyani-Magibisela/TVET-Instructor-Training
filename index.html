TVET Instructor Training

Welcome to Sci-Bono
We provide training programs to equip lecturers with digital literacy skills to develop a digitally competent workforce. Join us in our mission to improve the lives of South Africans through education and training.

Our Training Program

Our proposed training program is focused on equipping lecturers with essential digital literacy skills and basic e-teaching or didactical training/digital pedagogy. This will enable them to develop a digitally competent workforce capable of responding to fast-paced changes and the adoption of new technologies.

Lecturers will gain knowledge and skills to create engaging online learning environments, use technology to enhance the learning experience and effectively integrate digital tools into their teaching practice. The program is flexible and tailored to meet the needs of TVET lecturers.

Our training program is delivered by experienced trainers and we provide high-quality support to ensure lecturers get the most out of the program and continue to improve their digital literacy skills.
